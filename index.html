I was born in Jingjiang, Jiangsu in 1989 and am a member of the Jiusan Society. I received my Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. and my Ph.D. in Information and Communication Computing from the University of Technology Sydney University of Technology Sydney. in December 2018 and November 2020 respectively. I have published more than 40 papers in academic or conference journals, including nearly 30 SCI/EI papers as the first author or corresponding author, including IEEE TIP(3), IEEE TCSVT(3), IEEE TITS(1), IEEE TCE(1), IEEE SPL(2), IEEE PJ(1) as the first author. At the same time, I have applied for 9 invention patents, and 7 have been authorized. I am the deputy editor-in-chief of the academic journal IEEE SPL, the deputy editor-in-chief of the IEEE OJSP emergency conference, and the guest editor-in-chief of Electronics.

Research Interests

My research interests cover cyberspace content security, computer vision, aritificial intelligence, and machine learning, including:

  • Cyberspace content security: deepfake, digital watermark, tampering image detection
  • Visual Perception: object detection and recognition, crowd counting, optical flow estimation
  • Image processing: image/video restoration, image/video enhancement
